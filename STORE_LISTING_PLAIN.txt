English Description
============================================

Image Gen Prompter - Ultra-Detail AI Prompt Generator

Transform any image into detailed prompts for AI image generators with a single right-click!

KEY FEATURES

Multi-Platform Support - Generate optimized prompts for:
  - Midjourney v6.1
  - Stable Diffusion XL (SDXL)
  - Flux.1
  - DALL-E 3
  - All platforms at once

3 AI Engines - Choose your preferred AI:
  - Google Gemini (Flash) - Fast & Free tier available
  - OpenAI GPT-4o - High quality analysis
  - Anthropic Claude (Sonnet) - Detailed understanding

200+ Attributes Analyzed:
  - Subject identification & relationships
  - Face micro-details & expressions
  - Clothing, accessories & styling
  - Composition & environment
  - 40+ lighting types
  - Color science & mood
  - Technical camera settings

HOW TO USE

1. Right-click on any image
2. Select "Generate Prompt"
3. Choose your AI service and target platform
4. Get your detailed prompt instantly!

PRIVACY FIRST

- Your API keys are stored locally in your browser
- No data is sent to our servers
- Images are processed directly through your chosen AI API

REQUIREMENTS

You need at least one API key:
- Gemini API (free tier available): https://aistudio.google.com/apikey
- OpenAI API: https://platform.openai.com/api-keys
- Claude API: https://console.anthropic.com/
